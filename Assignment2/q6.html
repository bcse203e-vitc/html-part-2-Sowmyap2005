Vegetable Stir Fry

    Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

  • 1 onion, thinly sliced

  • 1 bell pepper, sliced

  • 1 carrot, julienned

  • 1 zucchini, sliced

  • 1 cup broccoli florets

  • 1/2 cup snow peas

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium)

  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar

  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup

  • 1 tsp sesame seeds (optional)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

Procedure

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  2. Add onion, bell pepper, zucchini, carrot, and broccoli; stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
    3. Add snap peas and mushrooms, cook for 3 minutes.
  3. Mix soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil; pour over veggies, stir for 2 minutes.
  4. Garnish with sesame seeds and serve over rice or noodles.

    Nutritions

  • Calories: ~280-320 kcal

  • Protein: ~10g

  • Carbs: ~35g

  • Fat: ~15g

  • Fiber: ~8g

Chickpea Curry


    Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed

  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes

  • 1 cup coconut milk

  • 1 cup broccoli florets

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

  • 1 tsp ground turmeric

Procedure

  1. Sauté aromatics: Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add chopped onion, garlic, and grated ginger. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add spices: Stir in curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and cayenne pepper (if using). Cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add chickpeas and tomatoes: Add chickpeas, diced tomatoes, and coconut milk. Stir to combine.
  4. Simmer: Bring the mixture to a simmer and cook for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Season and finish: Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice. Stir well.
  6. Serve: Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.

    Nutritions

  • Calories: ~350-400 kcal

  • Protein: ~10-12g

  • Carbs: ~50g

  • Fat: ~12g

  • Fiber: ~5g

Pasta


    Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (whole wheat or regular)

  • 1 tbsp olive oil

  • 1 onion, chopped

  • 2 garlic cloves, minced

  • 1 zucchini, sliced

  • 1 bell pepper, diced

  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved

  • 1 cup spinach leaves

  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan (optional)

  • Salt and pepper to taste

  • 1 tsp dried oregano or basil

Procedure

  1. Cook the pasta: Boil pasta according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables: Add zucchini, bell pepper, and tomatoes. Cook for 5-7 minutes until tender.
  4. Combine: Stir in spinach, cook for another 2 minutes. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss together.
  5. Season: Add salt, pepper, and oregano (or basil) to taste. Top with grated Parmesan (optional).

TIPS

  1. Prep Ingredients Before Cooking
  2. Use Fresh Ingredients
  3. Don't Overcrowd the Pan
  4. Rest Meat After Cooking
  5. Cook in Batches
